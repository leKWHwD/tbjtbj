How to create a faceless Instagram or Tiktok account to make Passive Income

January 18, 2024 • business

Ever wondered if you can grow on Instagram or TikTok without showing your face and still make some sweet passive income? Good news – it’s totally possible! Let’s dive into how you can create a faceless account on these platforms and turn it into a passive income stream.

1. Niche Down Without Showing Off

First things first, pick a niche that doesn’t require you to be the face of the brand. Think about areas like gaming, cooking, art, travel, books, hobbies, or pets. These niches are perfect for creating content that focuses on the subject rather than on you.

2. Brand It Right

Once you’ve got your niche, it’s all about branding. Instead of personal branding, use clear photos or logos that scream your industry. Your username should also reflect your niche. This way, your brand identity is tied to what you do, not who you are.

3. Content Styles for the Camera-Shy

There are tons of ways to create engaging content without showing your face. Here are some ideas:

  • Stock Videos: Use pre-recorded videos and piece them together to tell your story. (see below for our recommended stock video sites!)

  • Prop Style Videos: Think cooking shows with overhead shots or art tutorials focusing on the hands.

  • Screen Recordings: Great for gaming tutorials or walkthroughs.

  • Waveform for Podcasts: Share audio bites with some cool background graphics.

  • Graphic Videos: If you’re good with graphics, this could be a fun way to go.

  • Voiceover Videos: Share quotes or stories with just your voice.

  • Reshare/Meme Accounts: Curate and share memes or other content (just be mindful of platform rules about original content).

4. Quality is King

Remember, it’s not about the quantity of content but the quality. Make sure your videos are top-notch and eye-catching. This doesn’t mean they have to be Hollywood-level, but they should be engaging and well-crafted.

5. Value Pillars in Your Content

Your content should either educate, entertain, inspire, or relate to your audience. Think about how you can incorporate these elements to keep your audience hooked.

6. Learn from the Best

Take a look at other successful faceless accounts. What are they doing right? How do they capture attention? Use these insights to shape your own content strategy.

7. Be Subtle About Being Faceless

If you’re trying to avoid showing your face, do it subtly. Don’t make it the focus of your content. The aim is to make your content so engaging that viewers don’t even notice you’re not showing your face.
