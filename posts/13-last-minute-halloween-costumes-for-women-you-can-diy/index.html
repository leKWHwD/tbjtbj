13 Last-Minute Halloween Costumes for Women You Can DIY

August 23, 2023 • halloween

Halloween is just around the corner, and if you’re anything like me, you’ve probably left your costume planning to the last minute. But don’t fret! I’ve got you covered with 13 fabulous, easy-to-assemble, last-minute Halloween costumes for women. Trust me, you’ll be the life of the party without breaking the bank or your schedule.

1. Rosie the Riveter

What You’ll Need:

  • Blue collared shirt

  • Red bandana

  • Red lipstick

How to Assemble:
Roll up those sleeves, tie your hair up in a bandana, and swipe on some red lipstick. Flex those muscles and embody the iconic Rosie the Riveter! This costume is not just cute; it’s a powerful statement.

2. Audrey Hepburn in “Breakfast at Tiffany’s”

What You’ll Need:

  • Little black dress

  • Pearl necklace

  • Black gloves

  • Tiara or hairpiece

How to Assemble:
Channel your inner Audrey Hepburn with a classic little black dress, pearl necklace, and a tiara. Don’t forget the black gloves for that extra touch of elegance. Breakfast at Tiffany’s, anyone?

3. Witch

What You’ll Need:

  • Black dress

  • Witch hat

  • Broomstick

How to Assemble:
Wear a black dress, add a witch hat, and carry a broomstick. For added effect, you can even paint your face green. You’ll be casting spells all night long!

4. Catwoman

What You’ll Need:

  • Black leggings

  • Black top

  • Cat ears headband

  • Black mask or eyeliner for whiskers

How to Assemble:
Slip into some black leggings and a black top, add a cat ears headband, and draw on some whiskers with eyeliner. Meow!

5. Cowgirl

What You’ll Need:

  • Plaid shirt

  • Cowboy hat

  • Jeans

  • Cowboy boots

How to Assemble:
Yee-haw! Put on a plaid shirt, jeans, cowboy boots, and top it all off with a cowboy hat. You’re ready for the rodeo!

6. Ghost

What You’ll Need:

  • White bedsheet

  • Scissors

How to Assemble:
Cut out holes for eyes in a white bedsheet and drape it over yourself. It’s a classic that never gets old!

7. ’80s Workout Instructor

What You’ll Need:

  • Bright-colored leotard

  • Leg warmers

  • Headband

How to Assemble:
Time to get physical! Wear a bright-colored leotard, add leg warmers and a headband, and you’re ready to lead the Halloween workout session.

8. Minnie Mouse

What You’ll Need:

  • Red dress with white polka dots

  • Mouse ears headband

  • Yellow shoes

How to Assemble:
Put on a red dress with white polka dots, add a mouse ears headband, and yellow shoes. You’re the cutest mouse in the house!

9. Pirate

What You’ll Need:

  • White blouse

  • Black skirt or pants

  • Bandana

  • Eye patch

How to Assemble:
Ahoy, matey! Wear a white blouse with a black skirt or pants, tie a bandana around your head, and add an eye patch. You’re ready to sail the seven seas!

10. Vampire

What You’ll Need:

  • Black dress

  • Red lipstick

  • Vampire fangs

How to Assemble:
Slip into a black dress, apply some red lipstick, and add vampire fangs. You’re ready to take a bite out of Halloween!

11. Greek Goddess

What You’ll Need:

  • White bedsheet or long white dress

  • Gold belt or rope

  • Gold leaf headband

How to Assemble:
Drape yourself in a white bedsheet or long white dress, cinch it at the waist with a gold belt or rope, and add a gold leaf headband. You’re divine!

12. Flapper

What You’ll Need:

  • Fringe or sequin dress

  • Feather headband

  • Pearl necklace

  • Black gloves

How to Assemble:
Step back into the Roaring ’20s with a fringe or sequin dress, feather headband, pearl necklace, and black gloves. You’ll be the bee’s knees!

13. Social Media Influencer

What You’ll Need:

  • Trendy outfit

  • Smartphone

  • Optional: Ring light

How to Assemble:
Put on your most Instagram-worthy outfit, grab your smartphone, and if you have a ring light, bring it along. You’re ready to go viral!

There you have it, folks! Thirteen last-minute Halloween costumes that are easy to put together and sure to impress.
