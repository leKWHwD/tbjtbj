The easiest way to create a digital planner that syncs with Google Calendar

January 7, 2024 • digital-planning

I’m thrilled to share an exciting update from Planify Pro that’s going to make your digital planning experience even more seamless and efficient. Planify Pro has introduced a fantastic feature that allows you to add Google Calendar links to your digital planner with just one click. Yes, you heard that right – it’s automatic and incredibly user-friendly!

Try Planify Pro!

Effortless Integration of Google Calendar Links

Planify Pro’s latest update means you no longer have to go through tedious settings or type anything manually to integrate Google Calendar into your planner. Simply activate the Google links in the widgets, and they will automatically be incorporated into your planner upon export. This feature is a game-changer for those who rely heavily on Google Calendar for their scheduling needs.

Give Planify Pro a try!

Customization at Your Fingertips

The beauty of this feature lies in its customization options. For instance, if you have a monthly calendar in your planner, you can easily add a Google Calendar link. In the settings, you’ll find an option called ‘Google link calendar.’ Here, you can choose the link’s position (right, top, left, bottom, etc.) and even select a color for the link. If you prefer a more subtle look, you can set the link to ’transparent,’ making it invisible yet fully functional.

Icons for Easy Identification

Planify Pro has also introduced three different icons for you to choose from, adding a visual element to your planner. This makes it easier to identify your Google Calendar links at a glance.

Save time with Planify Pro!

Daily Schedule Widget with Google Links

Another fantastic addition is the integration of Google links in the daily schedule widget. This widget, known as the ’time schedule widget,’ now supports Google links specific to the timing. For example, if you have a date flagged for November 30th and you click on 11 AM in your planner, it will open up the Google Calendar for that specific time. The customization options for color and transparency apply here as well, allowing you to tailor the appearance to your liking.

Future Updates and Support

The team at Planify Pro is continuously working to enhance your planning experience. They plan to add Google links to other widgets and even to the date flags. This means more flexibility and integration options for your digital planner.

Conclusion

Planify Pro’s integration of Google Calendar links is a significant step forward in digital planning. It simplifies the process, offers customization, and enhances the overall functionality of your digital planner.
