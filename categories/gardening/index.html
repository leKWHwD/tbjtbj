What are annual flowers

April 16, 2023 • gardening

Annual flowers are one of the most important factors in a gardener’s toolkit. They provide color and interest to the garden for the entire growing season. Many of these plants are easy to grow and care for, making them perfect for novice gardeners. In this article, we will introduce you to some of the most popular annual flowers, as well as provide tips on how to care for them.

What are annual flowers?

Annual flowers are those that complete their life cycle in one growing season. This means that they sprout from seed, bloom, produce seeds, and die–all within a single year. Many gardeners love annual flowers because they can provide a burst of color and interest to any garden or outdoor space. Plus, since they only last for one season, you can change up your blooms each year to keep things looking fresh. Some popular annual flowers include impatiens, petunias, marigolds, and zinnias.
