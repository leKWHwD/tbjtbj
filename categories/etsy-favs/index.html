If you’ve been searching for a way to make passive income, you’re in the right place. Today, we’re diving deep into the world of PLR digital planners available on Etsy. But first, let’s clear up a couple of things.

What is PLR?

PLR stands for “Private Label Rights.” In simple terms, it means you can purchase a product, like a digital planner, and have the rights to rebrand and resell it as your own. Imagine buying a cake mix, adding your own special ingredients, and then selling it as your unique recipe. That’s PLR in a nutshell!

Why Use PLR?

  1. Time-Saver: Creating products from scratch can be time-consuming. With PLR, the groundwork is already done for you.

  2. Cost-Effective: Hiring designers or spending hours designing can be expensive. PLR products are usually affordable.

What is a “witchy aesthetic?

A “witchy” aesthetic often includes elements of nature, such as plants, herbs, and crystals. It may also incorporate items associated with witchcraft and magic, such as tarot cards, cauldrons, and broomsticks. The color palette is often dark and muted, with shades of black, purple, and deep red. Textures like velvet, suede, and lace are common. The overall vibe is mysterious, with a hint of eeriness and a touch of elegance.

What are some “witchy” imagery?

Decorations like dried flowers, vintage apothecary jars, and candles are also popular in a witchy aesthetic. The imagery used in this aesthetic is often inspired by the occult, like astrology and alchemy symbols, and often featuring imagery of the moon and stars.
