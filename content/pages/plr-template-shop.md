## What is PLR?

One of the best things about our **PLR Canva templates** is that they come with **private label rights**, meaning that you can use them as many times as you want and even sell them to others.

## What is MRR?

  
It stands for **Master Resell Rights**. Basically, it's like buying a cool digital product, like an ebook or course, at a special price and then **being able to sell it as your own**. It's like having your own mini-store without all the hassle of creating the product yourself.

Here's the kicker: **you keep all the profits!** No need to share with the original creator. Plus, you can **even rebrand the product** to fit your style and **sell the resell rights** to others, building a whole network of sellers. Pretty cool, right?
