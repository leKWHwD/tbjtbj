#### What is the Planner Lovin' Library?

This digital library is made especially for digital planners!

**Starting out and don't know what suits you?**

**Don't want to spend your time searching for new stickers and inserts?**

We've got you covered!

For a small fee, I'll be sharing my personal collection of **functional and decorative stickers, inserts, notebooks** and **other** creative resources I've created over the years!

With this library, you can figure out your planning style right from the start and not waste time looking for stickers and inserts.

Each month, **the library will grow** with new stickers sets, inserts and notebooks!

Done with the library? You can cancel anytime!

#### Can I use these for printable planning?

Yes!

Our sets can be compatible with printable planning.

- With **individually cropped PNGs**, you can add them to any document like Google Docs as images and resize to your liking
- With **inserts** and PDF based pages, you can resize them in your printer settings to the size you'd like (See instructions below)
