## What's the story behind your shop?

I studied to be a pharmacist, but after 2 years into the job I got a burn out. Also I got pregnant with our beautiful daughter and after a tough pregnancy and labor we decided I should stay at home with the little one and do what I love, which has been planning and crafting. Thats when I opened up my planner shop :)

## Where can we find your shop?

[Etsy](https://www.etsy.com/shop/psbykim/)

[Website](http://Www.psbykim.com)

## What kind of items do you sell in your shop?

Physical Planner Items

## What is the inspiration behind your designs?

I love a lot of themes and colorways, but also minimal designs lately. I come up with ideas and make stuff on the go. Some are a hit, others a miss.

![](images/rsz_1ontwerp_zonder_titel_39-3.png)

## What is your bestseller?

My plastic envelopes for Happy Planners. I havent seen other shops selling them and they are personalizable with a vinyl sticker.

![Clear sticker storage pouch w/flap - Classic Happy Planner](images/il_794xN2908489291_3wiy.jpg)

## What is your favourite planning/journaling tip?

Don't be hard on yourself when you can't commit to your planner. It has to be relaxing and not a must do when you don't feel like it.
