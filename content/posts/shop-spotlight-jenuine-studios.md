## What's the story behind your shop?

My shop started out as a hobby project to share digital planning assets online, specifically OneNote planners and templates. I've used OneNote extensively as a student, but I found that not a lot of people knew about it. That's how I created my very first product: the OneNote Student Planner.

I am looking to design and release more OneNote planners and templates that suit a greater variety of users, and eventually venture off to explore other forms of digital planning.
