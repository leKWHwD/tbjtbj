## Why should you start a shop on Etsy?

With the increasing popularity of online shopping, Etsy has become a go-to platform for customers seeking unique and handmade items.

In fact, Etsy had 4.4 million active sellers and 90 million active buyers as of the end of 2020. Furthermore, Etsy has a global reach, which means that your products can be sold to customers from all over the world.

Etsy also provides a user-friendly platform that makes it easy to set up and manage your shop, with built-in features such as customizable storefronts, inventory management tools, and analytics.

If you're a creative entrepreneur, this might be the easiest way to make some money!
