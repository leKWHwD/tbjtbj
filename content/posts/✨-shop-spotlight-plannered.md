## What's the story behind your shop?

We started Plannered because we love the digital planning community. I’ve been a designer of digital stickers for a few years now and I found the best people are here. When we decided to build a marketplace, it was an easy decision. Because as much as we love Etsy, it’s hard to find what you are looking for when it comes to digital planners. Looking for an undated, horizontal planner with bonus stickers is hard to find in-between the printable, physical planners and the ads. So for us it was about making a search filter that works.

We also found that new designers as well as established ones often get lost in the void of Etsy’s algorithms. We wanted to give amazing designers a chance to show their stuff in front of the right audience without fighting for space.

![](images/01-ec65efda-1500x1125.png)

## What can we look forward to next?

We have over 1000 products and are growing daily, our designers are key to building a marketplace that works for them. We have some great upgrades in the future for both the designers and their audience.
