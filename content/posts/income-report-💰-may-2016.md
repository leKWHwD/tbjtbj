So… I’ve been seeing these around a lot.  I always wonder why people would post their income report and …I still wonder.  There may be many reasons, but I want to start posting mine because I want to reflect on my growth (or lack of) each month – and hopefully this exercise will push me to stay on top of my bookkeeping!! So here goes…

## MAY INCOME

Etsy Income: $78.17  
Shopify Store: $106.38  
Collaboration revenue: $662.05

**Total Income: $846.60**

## MAY EXPENSES

Shopify hosting\*: $148.96  
Paypal fees: $6.68  
Affiliate commission: $4.54  
Advertising and promotion: $55.48

**Total Expenses: $215.66** (Anything with an \* next to it is an affiliate link)

## Net Profit: $630.97

As you can see, I’m pretty small peanuts compared to other people out there.  I might also note that this has been one of the “good” months because some payments only came through now from last month.  So this is also a good lesson that what you see on someone’s inflated monthly income can be a big deceiving sometimes.

I added some Shopify apps this month and will be going on vacation next month, so we’ll see how June turns out! With a busy schedule and a side hustle like this, it’s hard to stay focused sometimes and do the things that bring in the best ROI.  But I think at it’s still a win when your net profit is not in the negative right??

Stay tuned for next month!
