## What is a digital planner?

A digital planner is a PDF planner that helps you keep track of your schedule and to-do list. It can be used on an iPad, tablet, or Android phone. You can use a PDF annotation app like Goodnotes to write down appointments and reminders for yourself, and it can be synced across all your devices.
