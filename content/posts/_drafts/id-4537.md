## What is a garden zone?

A garden zone is a geographical area in which plants will thrive. The USDA Plant Hardiness Zone Map divides the United States into eight zones, each with its own optimum range of temperatures and amounts of rainfall.

Some plants that grow well in zone 10 include lilies, ferns, and azaleas. These plants need less humidity than plants in zone 1, but they still need enough to avoid becoming dry and wilted.
