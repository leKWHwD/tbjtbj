Are you looking to boost your productivity with digital planning? OneNote, a versatile note-taking app, can be your go-to tool for organizing your life digitally. Here are some top tips and tricks to make the most out of OneNote as your digital planner.

## 1\. Master the Hierarchy System

OneNote's hierarchy system is a game-changer. It allows you to create notebooks, sections, and pages, organizing your thoughts and plans efficiently. Think of it as a digital binder where you can categorize your notes for easy access. This structure is perfect for breaking down big projects into manageable tasks.

## 2\. Sort Your Pages

Sorting pages is a nifty feature, especially in the iOS version of OneNote. You can sort your pages alphabetically, by date created, or date modified. This feature helps keep your most relevant notes at the top, saving you time and keeping you focused on current tasks.

## 3\. Typing vs. Handwriting

OneNote's flexibility in allowing both typing and handwriting is a huge plus. Whether you're on your phone jotting down quick notes or at your desk typing out detailed plans, OneNote adapts to your needs. Plus, the ability to align typed text with notebook lines keeps everything neat and organized.

## 4\. Utilize Full-Screen Mode

Maximize your workspace by using the full-screen mode. This feature helps eliminate distractions by hiding the navigation bar, allowing you to focus solely on your notes. It's particularly useful during meetings or deep planning sessions.

## 5\. Side-by-Side Note Viewing

Ever needed to view two sets of notes simultaneously? OneNote lets you open a new window to view different pages or notebooks side by side. This is incredibly useful for comparing notes or transferring information from one section to another.

## 6\. Dark Mode and Visibility

If you're a fan of dark mode, OneNote has you covered. However, sometimes your notes might not be visible across all devices in this mode. The solution? Switch the background to ensure your notes are visible everywhere, maintaining the aesthetic and functionality of dark mode.

## 7\. Hyperlinking Within OneNote

Hyperlinks in OneNote can turn your planner into a powerful indexing tool. You can link to different pages, sections, or even specific paragraphs. This feature is perfect for referencing related notes or keeping track of related tasks and projects.

## 8\. Convert Handwritten Notes to Text

OneNote can convert your handwritten notes to text, which is particularly useful if you prefer writing by hand on mobile devices. This feature enhances readability and organization, making it easier to share and collaborate.

## 9\. Integrating Tasks with Outlook

For those who use Outlook, integrating tasks from OneNote to Outlook can streamline your workflow. You can set reminders and due dates in Outlook directly from your OneNote tasks, ensuring you never miss a deadline.

## 10\. Integrating Meeting Details from Outlook

Planning meetings in OneNote is a breeze. You can import meeting details from Outlook into OneNote, allowing you to prepare and organize your thoughts before the meeting. This integration ensures all your meeting notes are in one place.

## 11\. Printing Your Planner Pages

Need a hard copy of your plans? OneNote lets you print your pages as PDFs. This is great for those who like to have a physical copy for reference or for sharing plans with others who might not use OneNote.

## 12\. Emailing Your Notes

Finally, sharing your digital notes is easy with OneNote. You can email your pages as PDF attachments directly from the app. This feature is handy for sharing meeting notes or collaborating on projects.

Using OneNote as a digital planner can revolutionize the way you organize your life. With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to becoming a OneNote pro, making your planning process more efficient and effective. Happy planning! 📝🗓️
