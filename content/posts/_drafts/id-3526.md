While you might think that scrapbooking is a thing of the past, there are actually tons of great ideas out there that can help you get started on your next project! In this article, we'll explore some of the best scrapbooking tips and tricks to help you get started.

## Different types of scrapbooking

There are so many different types of scrapbooking that it can be hard to decide which one to try. Here are just a few ideas to get you started:

1\. Traditional scrapbooking – This is the most basic type of scrapbooking, and involves simply sticking photos onto pages and adding some handwritten notes. You can make your pages as simple or as elaborate as you like.

2\. Digital scrapbooking – This is a great option if you’re not a very crafty person, or if you want to be able to share your scrapbooks online with friends and family. You can create digital scrapbooks using software such as Photoshop or PicMonkey.

3\. Project life scrapbooking – This is a popular method for people who want to document their everyday lives in a more casual way. Rather than creating traditional layouts, project life scrapbookers simply add photos and journaling to pre-made pocket pages.

4\. Travel scrapbooking – If you love to travel, why not document your adventures in a scrapbook? You can include things like maps, ticket stubs, and souvenirs in your travel scrapbook, as well as plenty of photos of course!

## More complex scrapbooking ideas

If you're looking for more complex scrapbooking ideas, try one of these:

1\. Use a variety of embellishments to create texture and interest on your pages. Try using ribbon, sequins, fabric, buttons, or other 3D embellishments.

2\. Incorporate mixed media elements into your scrapbook pages. This could include incorporating watercolors, paints, or other mediums into your designs.

3\. Create a mini album. This is a great way to document a specific event or trip in more detail.

4\. Try making a pocket page scrapbook. This type of scrapbooking is perfect for those who want to add in lots of photos and journaling.

5\. Get creative with your page layouts. Think outside the traditional 12x12 format and try something different.

6\. Use templates to speed up the scrapbooking process. Templates can help you achieve a professional look without spending hours on each page.

7\. Journal about your process as you go along. Scrapbooking is not only about the finished product, but also about the journey you take to get there.

## Conclusion

Whether you're a seasoned scrapbooker or just getting started, we hope these ideas have inspired you to try something new.
