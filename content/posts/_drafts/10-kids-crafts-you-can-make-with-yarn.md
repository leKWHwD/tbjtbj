## Easy and fun kids crafts you can make with yarn

There are a lot of easy and fun kids crafts you can make with yarn. Here are a few examples:

1\. Yarn lollipops: This craft is simple but fun. All you need is some yarn, a spoon, and some candy melts. Melt the candy melts in the spoon and put them in the yarn. Then let your kids play with it by sucking on the lollipops!

2\. Yarn snowballs: These are a lot of fun to make and even more fun to play with! You will need some yarn, a ballpoint pen, and some water. Mix the water with the yarn until it’s a smooth ball. Then use the ballpoint pen to make balls out of the yarn and throw them at your friends!

3\. Yarn butterflies: This project is perfect for little ones who love butterflies. You will need some skeins of different colors of yarn, scissors, and a needle. Cut the skeins into butterfly shape and then sew them together using the needle. Your kids will love creating these beautiful creatures with their own hands!

## How to choose the right yarn for your project

When you're choosing yarn for a project, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to make sure the yarn is the right weight and texture for the project you're working on. Second, you'll want to consider what kind of project you're making. If it's a simple knitting or crochet project, you may not need as much of a variety of fibers as if it's a more complex piece like a blanket or afghan. Lastly, think about the finished product you're looking for. Some people prefer softer yarns while others prefer harder ones. The best way to find out what works best for you is to try different types of yarns and see which ones work best for your particular project.

## Easy and simple projects you can make with yarn
