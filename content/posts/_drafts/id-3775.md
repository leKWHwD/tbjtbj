If you're looking for a way to generate passive income, selling digital products is a great option. With digital products, you can create something once and sell it over and over again. There's no need to worry about inventory or shipping costs - all you need is a website or online store where customers can purchase your products.

- printables

Creating digital products can be a bit of initial work, but it's well worth the effort when you see the money start rolling in. Plus, once you've created a few products, it becomes easier and faster to create new ones. And as your product catalogue grows, so does your potential for generating passive income.

So if you're looking for an easy way to make some extra money, consider selling digital products. With a little creativity and effort, you can start generating passive income in no time!
