If you're looking for some authentic traditional Chinese BBQ, you can find it at Chinatown BBQ in Chinatown.

This place is not only aesthetically pleasing, but it serves up authentic Chinese BBQ dishes the neighbourhood is so well known for.

If you're looking for some good Chinese family-style dishes, this is the place to be!
