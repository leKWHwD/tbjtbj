## How to make a cardboard flower

One easy way to create a beautiful card-board flower is to follow these simple steps. First, cut a small piece of cardboard to the desired shape. Next, use a craft knife to score the cardboard diagonally at both ends. Finally, fold the scored ends inwards so that they form a petal shape. Do not worry if the petal is a bit crooked – it will look more natural that way. Place the flower on a sturdy surface and trace around it with a pencil. Cut out the traced lines with a craft knife, then remove the flower from the paper template.

## How to make a cardboard star

cardboard stars are a fun and easy way to decorate your home or office. All you need is some cardboard, a cutting mat, and a pair of scissors.

To make a cardboard star, first draw a star shape on the cardboard with a pencil. Then, use the cutting mat to make cuts out of the star. Make sure to cut all the way around the star. Finally, fold the edges of the star inwards so that they meet in the middle.

## How to make a cardboard tree

One of the easiest crafts you can make with cardboard is a cardboard tree. All you need is some cardboard, a pencil, and a few pieces of wire.

First, cut two pieces of cardboard that are the same size. Then, draw a tree on one of the pieces of cardboard. Use the pencil to add details like leaves and branches. Make sure the tree is symmetrical and the branches are evenly distributed across the piece of cardboard.

Next, cut the other piece of cardboard to the same size as the first piece. Draw a branch from the bottom of one of the leaves on the first piece of cardboard to the top of the leaf on the second piece of cardboard. Make sure that both pieces of cardboard are neatly aligned when you're finished.

Now, fold each piece in half so that they form a V-shape. Then, poke two holes at the top of each V for wires to go through. Twist each wire around a pencil so that it forms an "X". Finally, attach each "X" to one end of a wooden dowel or stick.

## How to make a cardboard bird

You can make a beautiful cardboard bird with just a few simple steps.

First, cut a piece of cardboard the size of your bird's body. Then, draw a outline of the bird on the cardboard with a pencil. You can freehand or use a template if you want. Make sure to accurately mark the wings and tail feathers.

Next, start to fill in the details of the bird with colored pencils. Be sure to include all of the feathers, beaks, and eyes. Add some highlights to give your bird extra realism.

Once you're happy with the look of your bird, use a paintbrush or a sponge to add some final touches. You can use different colors to create different shades or patterns on your bird's body.

## How to make a cardboard boat

Cardboard boats are a fun way to spend a summer day. They are easy to make and can be customized to any color or style.

## How to make a cardboard cat

cardboard cats are a fun and easy craft that can be completed in just a few minutes.
