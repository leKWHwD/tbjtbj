## Activity 2: Create a Stick Fort

Are you looking for a creative way to keep your kids occupied during the summer? Why not try creating a stick fort! This simple activity is easy to do and can be done in just a few minutes.

To start, gather some popsicle sticks and place them in a circle on the ground. Your kids can then start building their stick fort by stacking the sticks up until it's high enough. For added security, you can put a tight knot at the top of the fort. Once it's complete, your kids can enjoy playing inside their fort without having to worry about getting hurt.
