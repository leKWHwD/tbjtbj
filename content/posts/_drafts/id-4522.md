## Transportation:

### from the airport

  
  
Taxi  
  
Basic rate to city centre by taxi is 35 E daytime, 50 E night time.  
  
  
Metro  
  
The train station is connected to the airport, but does require going up a level from arrivals (by elevator) and crossing over a pedestrian bridge.  
  
One way is 8 Euros per person  
  
  
Buses  
  
Cost 5 E

Voltage: 230V

Plug Types: B, B3, C, SE  
  
  
  

## Is tap water safe to drink?

Tap water in Athens is perfectly safe to drink, as in most main Greek cities, but if you visit any islands it's best to stick to bottled - it's widely available.
