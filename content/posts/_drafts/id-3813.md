There are a ton of different apps out there that can help you create a digital planner, but which ones are the best? Here are four must-have apps to help you get started:

## [Canva](https://thebeigejournal.com/Canva)

Using [Canva](https://thebeigejournal.com/Canva) to create a digital planner is a great option! It's free to use, and you can easily customize it to fit your needs. Plus, there are tons of templates available online that can help you get started.

Creating a digital planner with Canva is simple and easy to do. Just choose a template that you like, add your own content and photos, and then save it as a PDF or print it out.

You can also share it with others so they can see what you're up to and help you stay on track.

So if you're looking for a way to get organized, try using Canva to create a digital planner. It's an easy, free, and customizable way to keep track of your life and goals.

[Try Canva for Free](https://thebeigejournal.com/Canva)

## [Creative Fabrica](https://thebeigejournal.com/creativefabrica)

Creative Fabrica is a great place to find free digital assets for your digital planner. They have a wide selection of digital stickers, backgrounds, and other elements that you can use to personalize your planner. You can also find some great tips and tutorials on their website to help you get started.

 Plus, they offer a wide variety of freebies, like digital stickers, that you can use to add a personal touch to your work.

[Try Creative Fabrica](https://thebeigejournal.com/creativefabrica)
