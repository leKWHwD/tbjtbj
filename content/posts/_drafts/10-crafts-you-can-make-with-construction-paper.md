## How to make a crown

One of the easiest crafts you can make with construction paper is a crown. Crowns are popular party decorations and can be made in a variety of shapes and sizes. To make a crown, start by cutting a small hole in the top of your construction paper cone. Then, insert a straw or a piece of plastic tubing through the hole. Finally, poke some Styrofoam balls or other small objects through the straw or tubing to create the crown's jewels.

If you want to make a more complicated crown, you can try carving out different types of shapes from your construction paper cone. You could also use construction paper glue to attach different types of materials to your crown, such as feathers, fake gemstones, and sparkly sequins. If you're feeling creative, you could even paint your crown with colorful paint or glitter.

Whether you're looking for an easy craft or something more complex, construction paper is a great medium for creating unique and beautiful Crowns!
