## What is a Cash Envelope System?

A Cash Envelope System is an easy and effective way to budget and save money. Basically, it is a system where you create envelopes that represent different categories of expenses (e.g., groceries, bills, etc.), and then put all of your monthly spending within these envelopes. Once a month, you would then go through each envelope and figure out how much money was spent in each category, and adjust your spending accordingly for the next month. This system can be very helpful in tracking your spending and finding ways to save money. It can also help you stay organized and make better decisions about how to spend your money. For example, if you know that you usually spend $100 on groceries each month, but you have been budgeting to spend $75 each month, using a cash envelope system will help you stay within your budget without feeling stress-ful or deprived. Plus, it can be fun to see which categories tend to account for the majority of your spending (e.g., groceries, utilities, etc.). If you are looking for an easy way to save money and track your spending, a cash envelope system may be the perfect solution for you!
