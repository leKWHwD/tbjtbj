If you're reading this, you must be doing research for your first baby. Congrats! What an exciting journey you'll be going through (and is already experiencing!)

When I was going through this exact research before my first baby arrived, I had no idea what I was getting into. Needless to say, I'm sure you've heard a lot of wisdom from moms and parents before you, but things are probably not sticking.

Don't worry! this post will summarize most of the most improtant things for you so you don't have to remember them! (mom brain much?)

## Sleeping

newborn sleep is something! You've hard of people sleeping like a baby, but in reality, a baby doesn't really sleep that soundly. Here are some things to get you through those sleep deprived nights.

**Must haves:**

**Swaddle**

THE most important for a newborn! My favourite is the Swaddle up swaddle sacks. The best thing about swaddle sacks is that you don't have to worry about the swaddle blanket unravelling. These little babies are houdinis!

[![](images/q)](https://www.amazon.ca/Love-Dream-Swaddle-Organic-3-6kg/dp/B0088HVGHS?crid=1M0LY79NIC17J&keywords=swaddle+up&qid=1647067173&sprefix=swaddle+up%2Caps%2C153&sr=8-5&linkCode=li2&tag=fieldnotesyvrcan-20&linkId=7e1c58029ff9227e2f5ba92654e848ad&language=en_CA&ref_=as_li_ss_il)![](images/ir) 

**Basinet/ Playpen**

Newborns can sleep anywhere, so it's best to have something that's portable so they can sleep where you can watch them. Playpens are great because they can grow with the baby and some also have a changing pad right beside it for each night changes.
