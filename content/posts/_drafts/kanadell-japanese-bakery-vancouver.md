Have you seen anything cuter than these buns?

This cute Japanese bakery is located in East Vancouver and specializes in these cute animal buns, Japanese styled bakery items like curry buns and sandwiches.

This bakery has a cafe section where you can sit down and enjoy your drink and pastries. Just keep in mind, they're not open everyday, so be sure to check their opening hours before going!
